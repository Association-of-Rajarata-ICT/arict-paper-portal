# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and respectful community. By participating in this project, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Use inclusive and professional language.
- Focus on constructive feedback.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insults, or disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers at:

- contact@arict-portal.example

All reports will be reviewed and handled with care.
