# Code of Ethics

This project supports academic integrity and responsible use of educational materials.

## Principles

- **Integrity:** Do not upload or share content you do not have rights to distribute.
- **Accuracy:** Help keep resources accurate and properly categorized.
- **Privacy:** Do not share personal or sensitive information.
- **Respect:** Treat community members with fairness and professionalism.
- **Accessibility:** Aim to make content usable for everyone.

## Reporting Concerns

If you see content or behavior that violates these principles, notify the maintainers:

- contact@arict-portal.example
